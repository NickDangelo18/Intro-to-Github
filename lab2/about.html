About the world of Dungeons and Dragons

Dungeons and dragons is a table top roleplaying game where 2-8 individuals make their characters rolling dice and participate in the world provided to them by the DM or Dungeon Master.

This however isn't the only thing Dungeons and Dragons is used for. Over the last few years it has been used to help kids with social anxiety as well as help them with things such as math and public speaking.
