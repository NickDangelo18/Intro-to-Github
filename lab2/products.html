There are few things your really need to play Dungeons and Dragons. The most important things are Dice and the three starter books every player should have. The Player's Handbook shows how to make characters and has the general rules in the game. The monster manual shows the enemies. The dice is like the controller of the game, and if your the DM the Dungeon Master's Guide helps you develop a campaign or world.

There are many more things available to buy like dungeon tiles and even soundtracks to further develop the immersion of being in a fantasy world.
